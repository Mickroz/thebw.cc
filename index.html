Alliance

[BWCC] is a proud member of the HLL Alliance.
The HLL Alliance primary goal is to help in the development of smaller/new teams by offering regular competitive matches - under the collective Alliance name, although where teams are represented by your own clan tag and identity.

While Hell Let Loose teams playing alongside each other is not particularly new, this project aims to centralise the organisation and communication via propagating Discord Announcements and further information between Alliance teams respective Discord servers, while also providing a private hub for new teams to discuss, develop and share ideas and experiences regarding Hell Let Loose strategies and tactics - which is particularly helpful for those teams starting out in Hell Let Loose or who are simply struggling to field players.

Enlist

BWCC (Barbed Wire Collectors Club) is a Hell Let Loose community. Camaraderie and teamwork are our core values. We’re looking for enthusiastic players of all experience levels and backgrounds to join us in competitive matches.

Most of our members are from the UK and Europe. Search for 'BWCC' in the enlist browser to drop in to a public match on our server, it fills up around mid-afternoon UCT.

What you can expect from us:


  • A welcoming community
  • A treasure trove of in-house HLL knowledge, we have some OG players
  • Regular chances to sign-up for competitive matches
  • Access to private test servers to build map knowledge, test out strategies, and do drill practice

What we expect from you:


  • You are 18 years or older
  • You have a working microphone and are willing to use it
  • You need only be as active in our community as the rest of your life allows.

Rules

Community Rules.

  1. Be respectful, civil, and welcoming.
  2. No inappropriate or unsafe content.
  3. Do not misuse or spam in any of the channels.
  4. Do not join the server to promote your content without permission
  5. Do not buy/sell/trade/give away anything without permission
  6. The primary language of this server is English.
  7. Discord names and avatars must be appropriate.
  8. Spamming in any form is not allowed.
  9. Controversial topics such as religion or politics are not allowed.
  10. Do not attempt to bypass any blocked words.
  11. Don’t ping without legitimate reasoning behind them.
  12. Alternate accounts are not allowed under any circumstances
  13. Do not advertise without permission.
  14. Any issues with other members try to sort out amicably, or Go through Admin to mediate.
  15. The Owner/Head Admin have final say in all matters Relating to the discord and game server.

HLL Server Rules.

  1. No toxicity of any kind.
  2. No abusing of the in game vote kick.
  3. No intentional team killing or revenge team killing.
  4. No excessive grief or trouble-making such as spamming voice communications, includes impersonating admin and music spamming.
  5. No recruiting for non [BWCC] communities on the server.
  6. No clan stacking, spread out evenly over the 2 factions.
  7. No cheating/hacking, stream sniping will result in a permanent ban.
  8. Commander & Squad Leaders MUST be active in voice communications.
  9. Solo tanks must be unlocked on request unless you have someone joining you asap (10 MINS MAX)
  10. Recon squads same as above.
  11. Treat all players with respect.
  12. Infringements of the rules will result in a KICK or BAN.

SEEDING RULES APPLY UP TO 40 ACTIVE PLAYERS !

  1. Fight only over the centre cap point, If you fail to comply to the seeding rule you maybe punished or kicked.
  2. Do not destroy enemy garrisons that are positioned outside the middle cap.
  3. If the middle cap has been captured, enemy garrisons can be destroyed, but only the ones over the middle point.
YOU MAY ADVANCE TO THE NEXT CAP POINT ONCE 40 ACTIVE PLAYERS HAS BEEN ACHIEVED.
